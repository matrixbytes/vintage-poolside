About The Villa

Vintage Poolside Villa offers a comfortable and relaxing stay for travelers visiting Pondicherry. The property features well-maintained rooms designed to provide a pleasant and homely atmosphere for guests.

Located in a peaceful neighborhood of White Town, it is ideal for those looking to unwind while still staying connected to the city's attractions. Guests can easily explore nearby beaches, cafés, and popular tourist spots.
